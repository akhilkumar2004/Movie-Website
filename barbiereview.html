her Barbie in the real world. Barbie is set to embark on ajourney to find out who the Barbie owner was and meet them. However, while Barbie was gone.
Ken who tries to attempt to fall love in Barbie still manages to fail, and as revenge he decides to transforms Barbie world into Ken.
How will Barbie save the world of Barbieworld? Will Ken be able to find love with Barbie? I will note that yes, maybe Barbie must not be for everyone
