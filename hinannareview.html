Hi Nanna Review: In Nani We Trust, And We Believed


Rating:⭐⭐⭐⭐



Whenever Nani does a romantic film, or an emotional drama, he excels. And in Hi Nanna
it's no different. The chemistry being him and Mrunal Thakur, was perfect. The songs were
such a vibe. The last 30 mins though, defintely hit everyone. This surely was the most peaceful watch.
