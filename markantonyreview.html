Mark Antony Review: A Flop Combination = A Huge Hit


Rating:⭐⭐⭐1/2



A flop director, working with a struggling hero, will output a horrible film right? Wrong. This film
proved the haters that this film would not work. Although, the true hero was not Vishal, it was SJ Suryah.
Long live the SJ Suryah supremacy. LADIES MATTER AH? The whole unique concept of someone trying to call themselves
from the past, is quite something I didn't expect from the teaser. Usually Adhik Ravichandran films, would have adult
comedy and horrible romance, but this is different. It's a time machine gangster film, done right. Can I say the climax
was hilariously amazing! When Vishal came out with his anaconda turret? That's literally him after he finally made a good
after five years. Overall, I am really surprised! Welcome back Vishal!
