Jailer Review: THALAIVAR COMEBACK SOLLU MAAAAAAA!!!!!!!!


Rating:⭐⭐⭐⭐



Jailer is an action black comedy film written and directed by Nelson. I went first day first show with my family and my god it felt like
watching a film in India. The massive cutout, the cake celebrations, the whistles and cheering, it was truly a feast. Never did I expect to
see a massive fanbase in the USA for Rajinikanth. As a huge Thalaivar fan it's a dream come true! Jailer is definitely the comeback for not just
only for Rajini but for Nelson Dilipkumar. His last film Beast failed to set expectations for the genral audiences and emerged as a major failure.
But now he made Jailer a lot more better than his predescor. Rajinikanth was so raw in this movie, which I absolutely liked since we got to see him in a
different avatar. Can I just say this man never ages? HE'S 72! IT'S UNBELIEVABLE RIGHT? Anirudh's music and BGM definitely elevated, which is no surprise
composed by him. The biggest surprise were definitely the cameo roles, especially Shiva Rajkumar and Mohanlal's. The interval was definitely out of the box,
but the climax, the climax was GOOSEBUMPS! There were slight criticisms in the second half where some scenes were unwanted and I felt like Tamanah and Sunil's characters were a waste, since they didn't correlate with the story. If only Nelson fixed that, then this film would've been his best in his career. Other than that, Jailer is surely a winner for all audiences.
