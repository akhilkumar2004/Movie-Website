Kushi Review: Vijay Devarakonda's Average Comeback


Rating:⭐⭐1/2



Kushi is a romantic drama film directed by Shiva Nirvana starring Vijay Devarakonda and Samanatha in the lead roles.
I watched this film in Tamil with absolutely no expectations due to Vijay's lasat outting which is Liger. However, after
I can somewhat strongly say after 5 years, Vijay Devarakonda is back with a bang. However, I think that Vijay played it safe with this one
since it is a genre he is most suitable at. Sam's character was overhyped but eventually she didn't have any hihglight moments in this.
Also really upset she didn't dub her voice in this film. I guess she had to take a break due to her past health condition. The music was
really nice by Hesham. But major drawbacks included length, direction, rushed climax, and unecessary boring scenes. If they had fixed those, then Kushi
would be a lot better. Overall, safe comeback from Vijay, but expected a lot more from this.
