Maamannan Review: The Sociopolitical Issue


Rating:⭐⭐⭐1/2



Maamannan is a political drama film directed by Mari Selvaraj, starring Udhayanadhi Stalin, Vadivelu, Keerthy Suresh, and Fahad Faasil. A powerful first half
but with a slightly let down second half. Cast performances were pretty good. But Vadivelu holy shit man, what a raw avatar.Music and BGM were fantastic.
The interval block was totally bonkers man. When it’s Mari Selvaraj you know it’s going breaking glass and shit! The dog and pig symbolism meaning both good and
evil man! Mari Selvaraj knows his symbolism! If only the second half could be a tad bit better then this would’ve been legendary. Overall another successful film
for Mari!
