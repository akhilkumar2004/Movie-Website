Pathu Thala Review: Only for AGR and ARR


Rating:⭐⭐1/2



Pathu Thala is an action drama film directed by Obeli N Krishna, starring Silambarasan TR and Gautham Karthik in the lead roles.
This film is a remake of the 2017 Kannada film Mufti. Overall an average and boring film. The plus points were definitely STR's role as AGR
and AR Rahmans powerful music and BGM. While the visuals are nice for a gangster film, the films writing and screenplay felt off at some points.
Overall an average remake.
