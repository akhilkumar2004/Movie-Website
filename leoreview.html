Leo Review: Bloody Sweet Ride!


Rating:⭐⭐⭐1/2



Leo is a an action drama film directed by Lokesh Kanagaraj starring Thalapathy Vijay and Trisha in the lead role. The hype
for this movie was wild, since many people wanted to know whether this film was in the LCU or not. I will say this film is
not a proper Lokesh Kangaraj masterpiece like Vikram and Kaithi. If you want to know whether it is in LCU or not, do give
Leo a watch. The first half was incredible followed an average second half. Thalapathy's acting was incredible, and for once
I can say that it is his best acting since Kaththi. Anirudh yet elevates once again with unique BGMS and his songs. Sanjay Dutt
and Arjun Das were powerful but yet only had limited screen space. Also great to see my idol Trisha pair up with Vijay after 19
years man! Man they both look cute on screen. The cinemtogrpahy was amazing but the VFX felt ok for me. I felt the hyena CGI could
be better, especially the first 10 minutes. There were some dragged boring scenes especially in the second half, in which they could
have removed, but honestly Lokesh tried his level best. Rest in peace the box office records! Also f**k the censor board.
