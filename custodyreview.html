Custody Review: A Venkat Prabhu Misfire


Rating:⭐⭐1/2



Custody is an action thriller film, written and directed by Venkat Prabhu, starring Naga Chaitanya and Krithi Shetty in
in the lead roles. Venkat Prabhu usually delivers with unique concepts with some fun flavors, but this film was completely
different from his other works. The concept in this film was unique, but it was let down with writing and screenplay.
Expected a lot more comedic elements in a this film and also better music. Overall, a huge misfire from Venkat Prabhu.
I am really scared about what Venkat Prabhu will do for Thalapathy 68.
