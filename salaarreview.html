Salaar Review: Prabhas Comeback? Yes and No.


Rating:⭐⭐1/2


I originally had expectations for this film, but sadly it didn't hit me. I expected this to have KGF elements, but there
weren't any. I will say the visuals in Salaar, are actually better than in KGF. Too much violence, for what cost? Shruthi Haasan
literally thani canned, and really there was a weak villain. I honestly thought, Prithiviraj was the villain, but nope, he was a
close friend of Prabhas. Although, I will say, this is better than most recent Prabhas films. Prashant Neel, please make Salaar 2
bigger and better!
