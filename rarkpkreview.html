Rocky Aur Rani Ki Prem Kahaani Movie Review: Karan Johar Chemistry


Rating:⭐⭐⭐1/2


I didn't much hope from this film, mainly because it is a Karan Johar film, but after watching this, I
was really surprised. This felt like as if this was some vintage Bollywood family drama. Karan Johar really
took us back in time! Ranveer Singh was surprisingly good, but the real star was Alia Bhatt. God damn she is
hot. Music was pretty nice, especially What Jhumka. Overall a Karan Johar surprise!
