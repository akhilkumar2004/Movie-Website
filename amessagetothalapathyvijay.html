A Message to Thalapathy Vijay

Dear Thalapathy,

I heard the news. I just want to say a huge thank you for being the biggest Kollywood entertainer of
Indian cinema. Ever since the days when we watch your films in small theaters and on Sun TV, your acting,
charm, and style, would never fail to mesmerize us. You would often do all the good things for society and for
your fans. The speechs you give will always be remembered. The transition from Thalapathy to Thalaivar is a tough
transition. The transition from cinema to politics is a challenging level. Though I might not be a huge diehard fan,
you are true hero of Indian cinema. Though Rajinikanth is the Super Star, you might as well be. We will totally miss you from
the big screens, and remember all the films we would used to cheer and whistle upon. All the best for your political entry, and
make Tamilnadu great again!
