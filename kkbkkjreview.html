Kisi Ka Bhai Kisi Ki Jaan Review: Cringe Veeram Remake


Rating:⭐1/2


Kisi Ka Bhai Kisi Ki Jaan is an action film directed by Farhad Sahmji featuring Salman Khan and Pooja Hegde in the lead role.
The film is an official remake of the 2014 Tamil film Veeram. As someone who enjoyed Veeram, this film is a remake gone absolutely
wrong. I will say I will praise the action sequences and the inclusion of South India in this film which is fair game, but at the same
time has an injustice factor to it. For instance, the music of the movie is terrible, but what could be even wirse then adding a nursery
rhyme with a Telugu flavored vibe. Salman Khan delivered a somewhat cringe performance, and Pooja Hegde does nothing as usual. Villain
selection is really poor as they don't deliver the hype in this film. Overall, save yourself from watching this, and instead watch Veeram.
