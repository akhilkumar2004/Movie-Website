Maaveeran Review: Veerame Jayam!


Rating:⭐⭐⭐⭐



Finally Sivakrthikeyan returns to his full avatar! An interesting concept of a young cartoonist who hears a mind voice which predicts
each move Siva does based on the comics he’s written. First half of the film was full of emotions and comedy. Second half was a little
dull but had some serious punches. Can I say the action in this movie is wild?! Like the mocobot shots for each action scene was the
goosebumps element! Mysskins villain performance was top notch! I really can’t wait to see him in Leo. Music and BGM was pretty good coming
from a lesser known music director. Also can we agree the interval was BEST INTERVAL BANG FOR AN SK FILM? Madonne Ashwin knew what he was doing.
Truly the comeback for the prince of Kollywood! After Doctor and Don, Siva made another banger.
